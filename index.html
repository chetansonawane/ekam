Introduction

Now, let me address what you’re probably thinking – what, really, is this all about?

Stories…. That’s what I’m here to tell you. But whose stories you might ask… They’re yours I say. Your story. Their story. His story. Her story. Well, faces change; stories don't. We may don our masks of difference, but in this world, you and I are the same. So let yourself step into this universe, the universe of Ekam, led by the voices of generations past, present and those to come.

Karavali

Before we begin to explore this world, allow me to paint you a picture. Imagine rumbling grey clouds over rolling green hills.. the smell of rain beckoning, mixed with the sputter of fish frying.. drums beating to the rhythm of waves crashing against a shore.. as smiling faces welcome you home. This is Karavali. The coastal belt of Karnataka.

Stories

In this place, faces and cultures change just as quickly as landscapes and road signs do… And there is a story waiting in every corner. Stories of tradition, of legacy, of finding flight and discovering one’s identity. For no matter where we’re from, the cycle of life pursues us all… Karavali has watched as generations have grown up on her land and left in search of greener pastures. As she has watched lives go by, she has waited patiently to tell her tales.. Now is the time to listen.

Cast
